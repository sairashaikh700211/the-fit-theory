ABOUT US

Fitness designed around your life.

The Fit Theory is a personalized fitness consultancy. We started offline, coaching one person at a time — now we're bringing that same hands-on, no-nonsense approach to a wider community online.

OUR STORY

Built from the gym floor, not a template library.

The Fit Theory began as a one-on-one coaching practice — real clients, real kitchens, real schedules. Over the years we noticed the same pattern: most people don't fail at fitness because they're lazy. They fail because their plan was never built for their actual life.

So we built a consultancy that flips the model. We start with your body, your culture, your food, your stress, and your routine — then engineer a plan around it. No banned foods. No 5am cardio unless you genuinely love 5am cardio.

Today we coach clients worldwide, online, with the same depth as our in-person work. The tools changed; the philosophy didn't.
